About Us

A proud member of the self governing Sports Club Council, the Badminton Club is a cohesive group of Cal Poly students who come together to play badminton for fun and to represent the school at all intercollegiate competitions. Throughout the year, we meet several times a week to practice for competition within the Southern California Collegiate Badminton League (SCCBL) and various tournaments. Our goal is to share our love of the sport with the general populace that is generally misinformed about our sport. We welcome all players at any experience or skill level. We hold training and conditioning for all skill levels from how to hold a racket to opponent psycho-analysis.
