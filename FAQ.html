Frequently Asked Questions

    • Is there practice?
    • Yes, there will be indoor practice Spring quarter 2022 in the North Gym. Practice times will be Monday, Wednesday 7-9pm, and Friday, Sunday 7-10pm.
    • Will there be tournaments and scclb league matches this year?
    • We will have a tournament at UCI on Apr 30-May 01 and another one at UC Davis on May 28-29
    • How much does it cost to join? What are the fees used for?
    • Fees are $100 for the entire year or $40 per quarter. Each tournament will cost $40. This will cover the birds that we provide for practice, and the tournament fees will cover all travel costs such as hotels and gas money. We only accept cash or check, please make checks out to ASI. You are welcome to come to the first few practices to get a feel for the team. However, we will begin requiring dues from all members after the first couple weeks of school.
    • What kind of tournaments or school matches does the club participate in and when do these events take place?
    • The club participates in 4 tournaments during the school year: UC Davis Fall Open (Fall Quarter), UC Irvine Collegiates (Winter Quarter), League Matches in UCLA (Winter Quarter), and UC Davis Spring Open (Spring Quarter).
    • Are there tryouts?
    • We do not hold tryouts! We welcome players of all playing levels and experiences.
    • How do I join the club?

    • Visit Club Sport page, sign in with your Cal Poly account, and complete the form.
